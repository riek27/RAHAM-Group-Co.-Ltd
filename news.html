15 NOV 2024

RAHAM Group Co. Ltd Announces Major Expansion into East African Markets

Corporate News 3 min read

RAHAM Group Co. Ltd has unveiled ambitious expansion plans to extend operations across East Africa, building on its successful track record in South Sudan.

Juba, South Sudan - RAHAM Group Co. Ltd, the diversified African holding company, today announced strategic plans to expand its operations into Kenya, Uganda, and Ethiopia over the next 18 months. This expansion represents a significant milestone in the company's growth strategy and underscores its commitment to regional economic development.

The expansion will see RAHAM Group Co. Ltd establish regional offices in Nairobi, Kampala, and Addis Ababa, creating over 200 new jobs across the three countries. Each subsidiary will extend its services to these new markets, leveraging the group's integrated business model.

"Our success in South Sudan has provided a strong foundation for regional growth," said the Managing Director of RAHAM Group Co. Ltd. "We see tremendous opportunities across East Africa and are excited to bring our expertise in engineering, humanitarian supply, agriculture, and mining to new markets."

The expansion plan includes:

  • Establishment of regional headquarters in Nairobi by Q2 2025
  • Partnership development with local businesses in each country
  • Investment in local talent development and training programs
  • Adaptation of services to meet specific market needs

This strategic move is expected to increase RAHAM Group Co. Ltd's annual revenue by 40% within two years and strengthen its position as a leading diversified holding company in East Africa.

Ayiikos Engineering Completes Major Infrastructure Project Ahead of Schedule

Engineering 2 min read

Ayiikos Engineering Co. Ltd has successfully completed the Juba Road Rehabilitation Project two months ahead of schedule, demonstrating exceptional project management capabilities.

Juba, South Sudan - Ayiikos Engineering, a subsidiary of RAHAM Group Co. Ltd, has announced the successful early completion of the Juba Road Rehabilitation Project. The project, which involved the comprehensive rehabilitation of 25km of urban roads, was completed two months ahead of the original deadline.

The early completion was achieved through efficient project management, advanced engineering techniques, and the dedication of the project team. The rehabilitation work has significantly improved transportation infrastructure in Juba, reducing commute times and enhancing road safety.

"We are proud to deliver this important infrastructure project ahead of schedule," said the Project Manager at Ayiikos Engineering. "Our team worked tirelessly to ensure minimal disruption to the public while maintaining the highest quality standards."

Key achievements of the project include:

  • 25km of roads rehabilitated to international standards
  • Installation of modern drainage systems
  • Construction of pedestrian walkways and safety features
  • Creation of over 150 local jobs during construction

The project has been commended by local authorities and community members for its positive impact on urban mobility and economic activities in Juba.

25 OCT 2024

Rabbai Agro Launches Sustainable Agriculture Initiative

Agriculture 4 min read

Rabbai Agro has launched a comprehensive sustainable agriculture initiative aimed at supporting smallholder farmers and promoting climate-resilient farming practices.

Western Bahr el Ghazal, South Sudan - Rabbai Agro Co. Ltd, the agricultural subsidiary of RAHAM Group Co. Ltd, has launched a groundbreaking sustainable agriculture initiative designed to support smallholder farmers across South Sudan. The initiative focuses on promoting climate-resilient farming practices, improving food security, and creating sustainable livelihoods.

The program includes training in conservation agriculture, provision of improved seeds, and establishment of farmer cooperatives. Over 500 smallholder farmers are expected to benefit from the initiative in its first year.

"Agriculture is the backbone of South Sudan's economy, and supporting smallholder farmers is crucial for national food security," said the Manager of Rabbai Agro. "Our initiative combines traditional knowledge with modern agricultural techniques to create sustainable farming systems."

Key components of the sustainable agriculture initiative:

  • Training programs on conservation agriculture and soil management
  • Distribution of drought-resistant crop varieties
  • Establishment of farmer field schools
  • Development of market linkages for agricultural produce
  • Promotion of agroforestry and sustainable land management

The initiative has received support from the Ministry of Agriculture and Food Security and several international development partners. It aligns with national priorities for agricultural development and climate change adaptation.

Early results from pilot projects show significant improvements in crop yields and farmer incomes, demonstrating the potential for scaling up the initiative across other regions of South Sudan.

Alubol Mining Forms Strategic Partnership with International Mining Company

Mining 3 min read

Alubol Mining & Investment Co. Ltd has entered into a strategic partnership with an international mining company to develop South Sudan's mineral resources responsibly.

Juba, South Sudan - Alubol Mining, a subsidiary of RAHAM Group Co. Ltd, has announced a strategic partnership with Global Minerals Inc., a leading international mining company. The partnership aims to develop South Sudan's mineral resources while adhering to the highest standards of environmental protection and community engagement.

The collaboration will focus on gold exploration and development, bringing international expertise and investment to South Sudan's mining sector. The partnership includes commitments to local content development, skills transfer, and community benefit sharing.

"This partnership represents a significant step forward for South Sudan's mining sector," said the Managing Director of Alubol Mining. "By combining our local knowledge with Global Minerals' international expertise, we can develop the country's mineral resources in a responsible and sustainable manner."

Key aspects of the partnership include:

  • Joint exploration programs in prospective mining areas
  • Investment in modern mining equipment and technology
  • Development of training programs for local mining professionals
  • Implementation of international environmental and social standards
  • Creation of local employment and business opportunities

The partnership has been welcomed by the Ministry of Mining as a model for responsible investment in the sector. It is expected to attract additional international investment and contribute to the formalization of South Sudan's mining industry.

Initial exploration activities are scheduled to begin in the first quarter of 2025, with full-scale mining operations expected to commence within two years.
