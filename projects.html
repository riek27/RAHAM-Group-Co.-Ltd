Completed Projects

Successfully delivered projects that demonstrate our capabilities and commitment to excellence

Juba Road Rehabilitation Project

Engineering & Construction Completed: March 2024

Comprehensive rehabilitation of 25km of urban roads in Juba, improving transportation and urban mobility.

The project involved:

  • Asphalt paving and resurfacing
  • Drainage system installation
  • Road signage and markings
  • Pedestrian walkways construction

This project has significantly improved traffic flow and reduced transportation time for thousands of commuters daily.

Duration: 8 months
Budget: $2.5 million
Client: Juba City Council

Humanitarian Supply Warehouse Construction

Humanitarian Supply Completed: January 2024

Construction of a 5,000 sqm warehouse facility for humanitarian organizations in Central Equatoria State.

Key features of the project:

  • Temperature-controlled storage areas
  • Security systems and access control
  • Loading bays and logistics infrastructure
  • Office spaces and meeting facilities

The warehouse now serves as a critical hub for pre-positioning emergency supplies for multiple humanitarian agencies.

Duration: 6 months
Capacity: 5,000 sqm
Client: UN Humanitarian Response Depot

Commercial Agriculture Farm Development

Agriculture Completed: November 2023

Establishment of a 500-hectare commercial farm for crop production and livestock rearing in Western Bahr el Ghazal.

The farm development included:

  • Land clearing and preparation
  • Irrigation system installation
  • Crop cultivation (sorghum, maize, vegetables)
  • Livestock facilities construction
  • Processing and storage facilities

This project has created employment for over 200 local community members and contributes to regional food security.

Duration: 12 months
Area: 500 hectares
Employment: 200+ jobs created

Ongoing Projects

Current projects underway across our various subsidiaries

Nile River Bridge Construction

Engineering & Construction Expected Completion: December 2025

Construction of a 350-meter bridge across the Nile River, connecting previously isolated communities.

Project scope includes:

  • Bridge design and engineering
  • Foundation works and piling
  • Superstructure construction
  • Approach roads development

This strategic infrastructure project will significantly reduce travel time and boost economic activities between the connected regions.

Progress: 45% complete
Length: 350 meters
Client: Ministry of Roads & Bridges

Gold Mining Cooperative Support Program

Mining & Investment Expected Completion: Ongoing

Technical support and equipment provision for artisanal mining cooperatives in Eastern Equatoria State.

Program components include:

  • Mining equipment supply and training
  • Safety and environmental compliance
  • Market access and fair pricing
  • Community development initiatives

This program aims to formalize artisanal mining activities, improve working conditions, and ensure miners receive fair value for their production.

Cooperatives Supported: 12
Miners Benefitting: 500+
Partners: Ministry of Mining, International Partners
