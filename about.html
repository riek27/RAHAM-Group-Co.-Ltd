About Us

Learn about our history, vision, mission, and core values

Company Overview

RAHAM Group Co. Ltd is a diversified African holding company established with a vision to drive sustainable economic growth across South Sudan and the broader East African region. Our portfolio spans multiple sectors including engineering, humanitarian supply, import/export, agriculture, and mining.

Through our five strategic subsidiaries, we deliver comprehensive solutions that address critical needs while creating lasting value for our stakeholders, partners, and the communities we serve.

Our integrated approach allows us to leverage synergies across different sectors, creating a resilient business model that can adapt to changing market conditions while maintaining our commitment to excellence and ethical business practices.

History & Foundation

Our journey from inception to becoming a diversified holding group

2020

Foundation

RAHAM Group Co. Ltd was established with a vision to create a diversified African enterprise that would contribute to economic development across multiple sectors.

2021

First Subsidiaries Launched

Ayiikos Engineering and Hiba General Trading began operations, establishing our presence in engineering and humanitarian supply sectors.

2022

Expansion Phase

Mocha Enterprises and Rabbai Agro commenced operations, expanding our portfolio into import/export and agriculture sectors.

2023

Natural Resources Entry

Alubol Mining & Investment was established, marking our entry into the mining and natural resources sector.

2024

Consolidation & Growth

All subsidiaries demonstrated strong growth, and we began exploring opportunities for regional expansion beyond South Sudan.

2025

Strategic Partnerships

Formed key partnerships with international organizations and strengthened our position as a leading diversified holding group in East Africa.

2026+

Future Vision

Planned expansion into new markets and sectors, with a focus on sustainable development and community empowerment across Africa.
