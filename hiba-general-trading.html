About Hiba General Trading

Hiba General Trading Co. Ltd specializes in humanitarian supply chain management, providing essential procurement and logistics services to NGOs, UN agencies, and humanitarian organizations operating in South Sudan and across East Africa.

With extensive experience in challenging operational environments, we have developed robust systems for sourcing, transporting, and delivering critical supplies where they are needed most. Our network of suppliers and logistics partners enables us to respond quickly to emergency situations while maintaining the highest standards of quality and accountability.

We understand the unique requirements of humanitarian operations and are committed to supporting the vital work of organizations that serve vulnerable communities.

Emergency Response Capability

Hiba General Trading maintains emergency response capabilities to support rapid humanitarian interventions:

  • 24/7 emergency procurement and logistics support
  • Pre-positioned emergency supplies in strategic locations
  • Rapid deployment teams for emergency assessments
  • Established relationships with air and ground transport providers
  • Experience operating in conflict-affected and remote areas

Our team has supported emergency responses to floods, conflict displacement, and other humanitarian crises across South Sudan.
