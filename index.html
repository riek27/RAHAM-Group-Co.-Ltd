Who We Are

RAHAM Group Co.Ltd is a diversified African holding company with a portfolio of subsidiaries operating across key sectors including engineering, humanitarian supply, import/export, agriculture, and mining. Our mission is to drive sustainable economic growth while empowering communities across South Sudan, East Africa, and beyond.
