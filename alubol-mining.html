Alubol Mining & Investment Co. Ltd

Ethical Mining & Sustainable Resource Development in South Sudan

Gold & Mineral Trading

Alubol Mining & Investment Co. Ltd specializes in gold trading, mineral exploration, and mining equipment supply in South Sudan. We operate with the highest ethical standards, promoting responsible resource extraction that benefits local communities and contributes to national economic development.

Our gold trading operations connect artisanal and small-scale miners with international markets, ensuring fair prices and transparent transactions. We provide technical support and equipment to improve mining efficiency and safety while minimizing environmental impact.

Through strategic partnerships and investments, we are developing South Sudan's mining sector as a sustainable source of revenue and employment, aligned with the country's development priorities and international best practices.

Investment Partnerships

Alubol Mining facilitates investment partnerships in South Sudan's mining sector:

  • Joint ventures with international mining companies
