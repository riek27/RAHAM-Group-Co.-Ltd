Rabbai Agro Co. Ltd

Sustainable Agriculture & Food Security Solutions for South Sudan

Agriculture & Livestock

Rabbai Agro Co. Ltd is dedicated to transforming South Sudan's agricultural sector through sustainable farming practices, livestock development, and agro-processing. We operate commercial farms while supporting smallholder farmers to enhance food security and create economic opportunities.

Our integrated approach combines modern agricultural techniques with local knowledge to maximize productivity while preserving environmental resources. We focus on building resilient food systems that can withstand climate challenges and market fluctuations.

Through our various agricultural initiatives, we aim to reduce dependency on food imports, create employment opportunities, and contribute to the economic development of rural communities across South Sudan.

Crop Production

Diverse crop cultivation for food security and commercial markets

Staple Food Crops

Production of sorghum, maize, millet, and cassava to meet local food requirements and reduce import dependency.

Horticulture

Commercial production of vegetables, fruits, and other high-value crops for urban markets and export.

Cash Crops

Development of sesame, groundnuts, and other cash crops for domestic consumption and export markets.

Seed Multiplication

Production and distribution of certified seeds to improve crop yields and support smallholder farmers.

Livestock & Poultry

Cattle Rearing

Commercial cattle farming for meat and dairy production, incorporating modern animal husbandry practices.

Poultry Production

Large-scale poultry farming for eggs and meat, supplying local markets with quality protein sources.

Small Ruminants

Development of goat and sheep farming operations for meat production and breeding stock.

Animal Health Services

Veterinary services, vaccination programs, and animal health management for improved livestock productivity.

Agro-Processing

Grain Milling

Modern milling facilities for processing sorghum, maize, and other grains into flour and other products.

Dairy Processing

Processing of fresh milk into pasteurized milk, yogurt, and other dairy products for urban markets.

Food Packaging

Packaging of agricultural products to extend shelf life and meet market quality standards.

Value Addition

Development of processed food products to capture more value from agricultural production.

Sustainable Agricultural Practices

Rabbai Agro is committed to sustainable agriculture that protects the environment and supports rural communities:

  • Conservation agriculture and soil health management
  • Efficient water use and irrigation systems
  • Integrated pest management and reduced chemical inputs
  • Climate-resilient farming practices
  • Support for smallholder farmers through training and inputs

Our approach balances productivity with environmental stewardship, ensuring long-term sustainability of South Sudan's agricultural resources.
